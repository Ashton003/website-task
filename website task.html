pokemon (series)

Pokémon games are role-playing adventures where players, as young trainers, explore a world, catch and train creatures called Pokémon, and battle other trainers to become the champion, aiming to catch 'em all and complete their Pokédex, using turn-based combat with elemental strengths and weaknesses.it is very relyent on stratagy and team building with slight hints of exploreation

legend of zelda (series)

The Legend of Zelda is an action-adventure fantasy series centered on the hero Link, the courageous Hylian destined to rescue Princess Zelda and the land of Hyrule from the evil warlord Ganon (or Ganondorf), who seeks the powerful Triforce artifact. Players explore vast worlds, solve puzzles, battle enemies, and uncover secrets, with the recurring theme of courage, wisdom, and power battling for balance. While Link is the player character, Zelda and Ganon are recurring figures, often reincarnations, with stories varying but always involving saving Hyrule from darkness

minecraft

Minecraft is a popular sandbox video game where players explore, gather resources, and build anything they can imagine in a blocky, procedurally generated world, offering freedom in two main modes: Creative, with unlimited resources for building, and Survival, where you craft tools, fight monsters, and manage hunger to explore and build structures for protection.

terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox adventure game where players explore, build, craft, and fight in a procedurally generated world, blending elements of action, RPG, and platforming with limitless creativity in single-player or multiplayer. You start with basic tools in a pixelated world and can mine for resources, discover biomes, craft thousands of items, build elaborate structures, battle hundreds of enemies and massive bosses, and uncover secrets, making it a game of endless exploration and possibilities.
